Hong Jun-ho

Assistant Professor, Sungshin Women's Univ.

I'm an Assistant Professor in the Department of Convergence Security Engineering at Sungshin Women's University. My research interests focus on legal policy and compliance within information security, personal data protection, and industrial security. Prior to joining SSWU, I served as Director at the Korea Information Security Industry Association. I earned my Ph.D. under the supervision of Professor Seung-Woo Son, Director of the Korea Institute of Intellectual Property.
